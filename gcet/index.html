GOVERNMENT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY

(GCET),JAMMU

Government College of Engineering and Technology (GCET), Jammu, is a leading institution committed to excellence in technical education, research, and innovation. Established to nurture young minds into skilled professionals, GCET emphasizes not only technical expertise but also moral values, leadership, and creativity, contributing to India’s vision of becoming a developed nation. Located in Jammu, the historic “City of Temples,” the college provides a vibrant learning atmosphere with experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and active industry linkages. Its mission is to foster academic excellence, diversity, and community spirit while inculcating an entrepreneurial mindset among students. GCET continues to empower future engineers with knowledge, innovation, and ethics, preparing them to meet global challenges and serve society with distinction.
